How Arduino Nearly Killed Lockitron

And how Electric Imp may save it @KuyMainwaring, 2013 Oct 31st

I know Cameron Robertson - co-creator of Lockitron - through the Y Combinator alumni network. During the Crowdtilt crowdfunding meetup in SF, he and I spoke about the pressures and constraints of raising money on promises made to a community of buyer-backers and I made a strong case for my argument that crowdfunding is caught in a bubble. At the time, I was between startup ideas and had serious doubts about the viability of the crowdfunded hardware project model in the long term.
