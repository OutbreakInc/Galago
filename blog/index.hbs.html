The idea

@KuyMainwaring, 2013 Sept 23rd

I have a passion for electronics, and like so much passion, it's irrational. I can't explain precisely why I enjoy building electronic gadgets, tools or products and I have an even harder time justifying the time I spend on it, especially because it remains so difficult to build a business in this space.

It is clear, however, that the electronics product industry has some pretty major problems. The biggest and most obvious is that it remains an old-fashioned and exclusive industry in many ways, an industry that's hostile to the adventurous, underfunded and "startup-minded" explorer and the small team. Why is it so hard to get a datasheet for this? Why are these development tools so awful? Why is it so expensive to get things made? These are real problems - many of which I've faced building Galago - and I refuse to accept that they cannot be solved.
