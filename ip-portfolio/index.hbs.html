IP Portfolio

Logiblock licenses its intellectual property for connected product design. IP licenses are usually (but not exclusively) offered in conjunction with our consulting services. Learn about the major categories of IP below.

Bluetooth® Low Energy

Bluetooth® 4.0 Low Energy (BLE) is a rapidly-growing protocol for short-range wireless thanks to its good design and widespread inclusion in smartphones.

Wi-Fi® networking

Wi-Fi is the de-facto standard for ubiquitous high-performance Internet connectivity aroudn the world. Its inclusion in a new crop of connected devices has spawned the term "Internet of Things".

Low-energy design

Logiblock specializes in developing low-energy products which maximize the potential of Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity and provide maximum flexibility in product design.

Wireless mesh-networking

Often the best wireless network is the one that you don't even know is there. Logiblock's low-energy mesh network self-assembles and is invisible to the user.

High-reliability engineering

Whether it's home security or safety-of-life applications, Logiblock engineers products to be tough and reliable.

Over-the-air (OTA) firmware update

Firmware updates are a necessesity in today's fast-paced, competitive world. Logiblock draws from vast experience to solve this crucial problem.
