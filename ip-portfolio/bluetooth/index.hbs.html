Bluetooth 4.0 Low Energy (BLE)

The proliferation of Bluetooth Low Energy devices is driving a fundamental shift in embedded intelligence, connectivity and a new kind of human interface. Logiblock offers BLE design expertise for BLE hardware, firmware, apps and user interaction.

Concept: BLE as the new universal user interface

Bluetooth Low Energy is a lower-cost, lower-power and more flexible option than designing a display or buttons into a product. Its pervasive, always-on low-energy wireless link allows graceful, uncomplicated integration into smartphone apps. As such, those become the user interface for a new generation of products in the wearable, consumable, positional and deeply-integrated spaces. Logiblock has experience developing these products and can help you design your product to maximize this new UI paradigm.

Standard: Apple iBeacon

Apple introduced iBeacon, a specification built on top of Bluetooth Low Energy that provides an open, vendor-independent framework for near-field geospatial positioning, physical-context-aware software experiences and some NFC-like features. iBeacon offers a veriety of valuable benefits to iOS application developers and is currently usable with third-party libraries on Android and other operating systems, with many of the same benefits. Logiblock was an early implementer of iBeacon and can combine a this feature with any other Bluetooth services or functionality, boosting a hardware product with the ability to dramatically enhance mobile apps. Talk to us about how we can put Apple iBeacon into your design.

Platform: Nordic nRF51822/51422 SoC

Logiblock is experienced with designing circuits, PCBs and firmware for the Nordic nRF51822/51422 system-on-chip. We have applied both the QFN (chip) and WLCSP (silicon die) variants to several commercial and research projects.
